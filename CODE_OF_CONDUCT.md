## Code of Conduct

This project follows the spirit of the Contributor Covenant: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/

In short:
- Have and assume good intent
- Don't be a jerk

Some emphasis:
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Credit sources of content you contribute when not your own

The lead maintainer reserves the right to make final decisions on acceptable behaviour.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the project maintainers directly.

This policy may be revised at a later time should the project grow a large community.
